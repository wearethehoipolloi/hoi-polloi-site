Staking ADA
With The Hoi Polloi

ADA is the native token for the Cardano blockchain platform. ADA is a digital currency you store own your own digital wallet. Every transaction performed in ADA is permanently, securely, and transparently recorded on the Cardano blockchain. The ADA stored in your wallet can be delegated to a stake pool to earn more ADA.

Why Stake ADA With The Hoi Polloi?

How to buy ADA?

To earn rewards you'll need to own some ADA and delegate it to the Hoi Polloi stake pool:

  1. Buy ADA on an exchange like Coinbase, Kraken
  3. Get a Cardano wallet, Yoroi is great, but there are others like Daedalus
  5. Transfer your ADA from your exchange to your wallet. You will find your address in your wallet, with the money from your exchange to that address.
  6. From your wallet you can search for a stakepool to delegate your ADA to. You can find the Hoi Polloi by searching for the ticker HOPO.
